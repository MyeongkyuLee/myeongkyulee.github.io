Study of drivers' behavior characteristics under dangerous situation

period: March, 2020 - Decembmer, 2020

The project's goal was to find and determine an intentionality of accident made by deliberately to acquire insurance fee.
To finde the intentionality, we designed four scenarios which are reported often at NFS.
And we acquired perception time, vehicle data(e.g. steering, braking, throttle pedal), avoidance method and etc.
